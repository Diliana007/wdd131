Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a reservation?

While walk-ins are welcome, we highly recommend making a reservation, especially for evenings and weekends, to guarantee your table.

Can I bring my own games?

Absolutely! While we have over 500 games available, you're welcome to bring your own favorites to share with friends.

Do you host private events?

Yes! We offer private event packages for birthday parties, corporate team building, and special celebrations. Contact us for details.

Is there a cover charge?

No cover charge! We only ask that each person orders at least one item from our delicious menu during their visit.

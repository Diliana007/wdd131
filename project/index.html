About Us

At Board Game Haven, we believe that a table surrounded by friends, family, snacks, and games is more than entertainment — it's a recipe for connection.

Research supports this: a 2017 study in the American Journal of Play found that playing tabletop games can improve cognitive flexibility, reduce stress, and strengthen social bonds across age groups.

"When playing a game, the goal is to win, but it is the goal that is important, not the winning."

— Reiner Knizia

Our mission is to create a welcoming café where laughter is loud, phones stay in pockets, and every roll of the dice brings people closer together. Whether you're a competitive strategist, a curious beginner, or just here for the cake — you belong at our table.
